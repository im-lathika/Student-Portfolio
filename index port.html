A bit about me

I'm a second-year Computer Science Engineering student specialising in Cyber Security at Prince Dr. K. Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology. I like understanding how things work end to end — from how a webpage is structured and styled, to how the systems behind it are kept secure.
